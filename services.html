Affinity Test In Java

This simple app was made with the idea to help future students pick their ideal department of education for future studies. The concept is conceived in a way that future freshmen take an affinity test which contains thirty five questions.
Before taking the test, the user has to enter his/her name and e-mail address. Questions and answers are designed in a way that by following psychological analysis extract the best potential profession corresponding to the user's affinites. The user's name and e-mail address are stored and along with the test results, a file is created within a folder with a unique codename associated to the user's name.
Results contain all the answers and for which of the professions the user gave the most answers, thus which profession suits his/her affinituies most.

Data was collected and results were later sent to potential future freshmen. This app was tested at the Education Fair in Belgrade in 2017 and over 1000 users used this app in two days, which showed its potential use for universities and faculties.

Market analysis

This is one of the most important aspects included in the business plan. The results of the analysis are the starting point for evaluating the project's success in the market.

By saying that a market with consumers who need your product/service exists is not precise enough. Consumers have different perspectives on the same product/service type. Therefore, it's needed to investigate consumers' preferences as one of the steps in market segmentation.
Also, we need to bear in mind the facts such as segment size, expected growth in the future and the structural appeal of the segment. There are three main marketing strategies which cover the targeted market that can be considered.
Within the market analysis, a S.W.O.T. analysis is included. It can be intended for business purposes, or implemented in the field of personal development as well.
Along with it, additional analysis can contrubute to pointing out the critical aspects of business. Using P.E.S.T. analysis the environment can be thoroughly studied.

After all aspects have been considered, we can proceed to making a model for the campagin. This includes a 4C model and W.I.S.E. model as a variation for 4C marketing strategy.
